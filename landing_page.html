Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning (AI/ML) Team is where theory meets application. We take you on a journey from the very basics of AI/ML concepts and statistical foundations up to advanced techniques like deep learning, neural networks, and model deployment. Members gain practical experience working with data, building predictive models, and understanding the future of intelligent systems.                        

Web Dev

Architecting the digital frontier.

Web Development

Web Development Team is dedicated to mastering the craft of front-end development. We provide a comprehensive curriculum starting with the essentials: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (JS). Our goal is to equip members with the skills to build amazing, responsive, and interactive frontend experiences, turning design concepts into functional, engaging websites.                        

Cyber Security

Cyber Security

Cyber Security Team introduces members to the crucial world of digital defense and offense. We cover the basics of hacking and penetration testing, ethical security practices, network security, and vulnerability assessment. Learn how to think like an attacker to effectively defend systems and understand the critical role of security in the modern digital age.                        

Graphic Design

Visualizing the future.

Graphic Design

Graphic Design (GD) Team focuses on the principles of aesthetic and effective digital communication. Our primary focus is on User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UX) design, ensuring digital products are not only beautiful but intuitive to use. We teach members how to create compelling visual assets, including professional posters and informative infographics, mastering tools and techniques that bring ideas to life.                        

Comp. Programming

Computational Programming

Competitive Programming (CP) Team sharpens problem-solving skills through algorithm design and coding. We teach the fundamentals of Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) and progress to tackling complex challenges at an advanced competitive level. This team is for those who want to improve their logical thinking, coding efficiency, and interview readiness.
